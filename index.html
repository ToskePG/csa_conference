MSA Dubrovnik 2025 – Our Annual Conference

 

Dear everyone,

 

We are proud to announce that Montenegrin Sports Academy from Podgorica, Montenegro, will organize the 22nd International Scientific Conference in sports sciences which will be held during 03.-06. April 2025. in Cavtat, Croatia, next to historical Dubrovnik city in hotel Croatia. The topic of the upcoming conference is: "Sport, Physical Activity, and Health - Contemporary perspectives".

 

Together with out international partners we are looking forward to seeing you in April 2025. in Cavtat.

 

Welcome!

 

Prof. Duško Bjelica, Conference President

 

 

Important Update: Second Call For Abstract Submission

We would like to remind you of the upcoming 22nd Prestigious Conference of the Montenegrin Sports Academy, which will take place from April 3rd to 6th, 2024, in Cavtat, Dubrovnik.The deadline for abstract submission is 15.12.2024. We are eagerly looking forward to your potential contribution to this significant event.

We would also like to extend our sincere thanks to all the authors who have already submitted their abstracts and confirmed their participation. Additionally, we express our deep gratitude to our partners and collaborators for their invaluable support, which continues to make this conference a success year after year.

We look forward to welcoming you to Cavtat, Dubrovnik, for what promises to be an inspiring and productive gathering.
