Pay Online

To pay your registration fee online now, go to our online payments page (press the appropriate button) and follow the prompts. You can pay using Visa, MasterCard, Maestro and Visa Electron, and our preferred option is via debit card (instead of credit card) as these incur the lowest processing costs.

 

Paying your registration fee online is quick and easy, available 24 hours a day, and secure through 2CheckOut.com website as a payment facilitator for goods and services provided by Montenegrin Sports Academy. It also makes it easier to process your registration, so you will receive your receipt and statement of entry sooner.

 

You will need to send us any required paperwork separately.
