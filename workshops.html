Katjuša Radinović, Head of the Research Office


Katjuša Radinović is the Head of the Research Office at the Faculty of Sport, University of Ljubljana, where she oversees research activities and initiatives. She graduated from the Faculty of Sport, University of Ljubljana in 2010 with a degree in physical education. Since 2021, Katjuša has been taking care of the research activities at the faculty, coordinating and promoting projects that are in line with the institution’s mission to contribute to physical education and sports science.


Prior to her current position, Katjuša worked for over a decade at the Slovenian National Agency for European Community Programmes and International Mobility Programmes, CMEPIUS. Her work focused on advising and supporting European and international programmes to promote development and mobility in the sector.


Katjuša is the project leader of the Horizon Europe project SUPPORTER (SecUring sPORTs Education thRough innovative and inclusive Gender Equality Plans), in which the Faculty of Sport, University of Ljubljana is a project partner.


The SUPPORTER project is an European initiative dedicated to promoting inclusive gender equality within the European Research Area (ERA). SUPPORTER works with eight higher education institutions in Central and Eastern Europe to develop tailor-made, intersectional and innovative gender equality plans (4I-GEPs) that are inclusive and effective. The project explicitly addresses gender-based violence, including sexual harassment, and aims to create a safer and more inclusive academic and sporting environment.
